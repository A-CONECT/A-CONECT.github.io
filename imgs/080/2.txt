Facts:
The Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) estimates prescription drug spending will grow over 60% in the next 10 years.
Penicillin was accidently discovered by Alexander Fleming while he was cleaning up his laboratory workspace.
About two-thirds of American adults take at least one dietary supple­ment.
