Facts:
Business Insider suggests the best healthcare job is to be an Orthodontist (braces, jaw positioning) with an average salary over $200,000.
In 2017, almost 60% of Emergency Physicians agreed they felt burned out from the work they do.
2016 was the first year where physician private practice ownership was no longer the majority arrangement.
