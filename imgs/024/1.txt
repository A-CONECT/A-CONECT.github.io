A man proposing to his girlfriend on Mount Desert Island, Maine.
Facts:
About 75% of men think bending down on one knee is essential to a proposal. 
The majority of men will consult their girlfriend before buying an engagement ring. 
Roughly 2,000 couples will get engaged at Disney World each year.
