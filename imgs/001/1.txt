Topic: Native Americans Picture Caption:
Three Native Americans on horseback in 1905.
Facts:
There are over 500 Native American tribes in the United States.
Many states are named after Native American words or tribal names. For example, Michigan is named after the Native American tribe Michigamea, which means “great water.”
Many celebrities have Native American ancestry, including Elvis Presley and Oprah Winfrey.
