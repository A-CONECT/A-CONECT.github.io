Facts: 
During a race, a NASCAR driver maintains the same heart rate as a marathon runner.
The very first car race was held in France in 1895, with speeds up to 15mph. 
America’s first national car show opened in New York City in 1900. At this time, consumers favored steam engines over their gasoline-powered competitors.
