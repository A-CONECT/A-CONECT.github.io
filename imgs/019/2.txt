A family on a road trip to South America in their VW Westfalia van. 
Facts:
Yellowstone and Disney World are the two most popular road trip destinations in the U.S.
"I-Spy" is the most commonly played game on road trips today.
U.S. Route 20 is the longest road in the country, spanning nearly 3,400 miles.

(SM) 
What are some common things to pack for a road trip? 
What are some frequently played road trip games? 
How do you pass the time during long road trips? 
What are some pros and cons of going on a road trip?
(EP) 
Have you ever taken a road trip? Where did you go? 
Did you ever have an exciting or strange incident while on a road trip? What happened? 
Are there any stops you made that stand out in your mind? 
Have you ever gone on a road trip in an RV? What was that like? 
Have you ever gone off-roading? Tell me about it. 
(EX) 
Why do you think there is such a love affair with the idea of a road trip? What is the allure? 
Do you think that road trips are a good way for people and families to bond? Why or why not? 
Do you think that a road trip is an experience everyone should have? What leads you to think this way?
