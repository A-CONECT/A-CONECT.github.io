Facts:
The first speeding ticket was issued in 1904. The speeding car was travelling 12 miles per hour.
According to a 2016 study, Baby Boomers are 30% more confident than Millennials in their ability to add coolant to an overheated car radiator.
The longest traffic jam in history was in Beijing, China and lasted 12 days. Ironically, it was caused by construction trucks that were part of a road construction project.

(EP)
