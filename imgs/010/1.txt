Facts: 
Before the invention of erasers, artists used bread crumbs to erase mistakes.
The Mona Lisa painting doesn't have eyebrows or eyelashes anymore due to overcleaning. 
The father of the French impressionism, Claude Monet, disapproved of his son painting. He wanted Claude to take up the family business and become a grocer.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Have you taken painting or drawing class? 
Have you been to an art show/gallery? 
What art have you decorated your home with? 
Do you collect any paintings or drawings? 
How does art make you feel? 
What types of paintings do you enjoy the most (ex: landscapes, portraits, abstract)?
(EP) 
Was there a time when you witnessed a street performer? What kind of art were they creating (ex: handmade art or dancing)? 
Have you ever received a painting or drawing as a gift? 
Did you ever paint or draw something you were particularly proud of?
(EX) 
Studies have shown the importance of art in children's brain development. Do you think art classes should be mandatory in primary school? Why do you think schools typically cut art programs first when funding is short? 
Some pieces of art are sold for millions of dollars. Why do you think people are willing to pay so much? 
Making a living as an artist can be difficult. Do you think it is more important for someone to pursue their passion or seek financial security?
