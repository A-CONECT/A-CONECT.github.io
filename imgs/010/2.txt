Facts:
Most of Mt. Rushmore was carved with dynamite. This removed about 450,000 tons of granite from the mountainside.  
Greek and Roman sculptures were originally painted, but have since faded due to time and weather. 
The largest carved pumpkin weighed about 1800 pounds and depicted a zombie crawling out of the pumpkin.
