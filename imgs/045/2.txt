Facts:
Prince played 27 different instruments on his debut album "For You."
Metallica became the first and only band to perform on all seven continents after playing a concert in Antarctica called "Freeze 'Em All."
Canadian Astronaut Chris Hadfield released an album of songs recorded entirely in space.

(EX)
