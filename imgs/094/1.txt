Facts:
A little baking soda can help you grow sweeter tomatoes as a sprinkle on the plant's soil helps reduce its acidity. 
During WWI, 20 Hampshire sheep were used to keep the White House's lawn mown.
Hospital gardens were put in place to help patients heal; just five minutes of watching nature can reduce pain and anxiety.
