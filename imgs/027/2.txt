Facts:
Over the last two decades, the average age of babysitters has increased from 14 to 34.
A babysitter in a city makes an average of nearly $16 per hour, which is more than double the federal minimum wage.
Young female elephants who haven't yet reproduced are known to act as mother figures to young calves in the herd.
Possible Questions:
(SM)
Have you ever babysat before? 
What is the largest number of children you have cared for at one time? 
Have you ever used a baby sitter to look after your children? 
Do you like children?
(EP) 
Tell me about a challenging experience you've had with a child/children. What made this experience challenging? 
What kinds of things did you do in your community to make money as a teenager (ex: babysitting, paper delivery, landscaping, housesitting, etc.)? 
What did you spend your money on? What did you save your money for? 
(EX)
Do you think there's an age that is too young or too old to safely look after children? 
Some states have laws concerning how old a child must be before they can be left alone. How old do you think children should be before they can be left alone? Why is that? 
It takes a lot of time, effort, and money to provide successful child care. Do you think child care costs are reasonable or too expensive? Why is that? What are some ways child care can be made more affordable? 
If family members assist with child care, should they be compensated or do it for free?
