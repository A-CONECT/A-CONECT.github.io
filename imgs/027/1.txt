Facts:
In 2016, the average amount spent on a birthday party for an elementary aged child was over 500 dollars.
The most popular birthday cake flavor is Chocolate. 
Many ancient cultures believed the smoke from blown out candles would carry their prayers and wishes up to heaven. 
Possible Questions:
(SM)
Do you celebrate your birthday each year? 
What are some common things you do for your birthday? 
What are some things you've done to celebrate your birthday? 
(EP)
What do you recall as your most memorable birthday gift? 
Do you have any memories of receiving a birthday gift that you did not like? Tell me about that. 
How did you celebrate your birthday growing up? 
What was your favorite age? What significance did that age hold for you?
(EX)
Birthdays celebrations are not important to some people. Are birthday celebrations valuable or significant to you? Why/why not? Why might some people choose to/not to celebrate their birthday each year? 
Do you think it is more meaningful if someone spends a lot of time making a gift or buys an expensive gift for you? 
Do you think it's necessary to buy a gift to show someone that you care about them?
