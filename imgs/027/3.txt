A family enjoying their summer vacation at the beach.
Facts:
Television shows used to only play reruns during the summer months since they believed everyone would be outside enjoying the weather.
Frisbees became popular in the 40s, when college students started throwing pie plates around.
60% of children feel closer to their grandparents after they go on a trip together.
Possible Questions:
(SM)
Did you take summer vacations? Where did you go? 
What are some common summer activities that you enjoy? 
What is your favorite season of the year? 
Has your opinion of summer changed from childhood to adulthood? How so?
(EP)
Do you remember a summer vacation that you didn't enjoy? Why didn't you enjoy it? 
Did you attend school year-round or did you have a summer vacation each year? 
What activities did you do to fill your summer break from school (ex: camps, summer job, travel, etc.)? 
What was your favorite thing about Summer vacation? 
(EX)
Some people enjoy taking summer road trips each year. Do you think road trips are fun? Why/why not? Why you think some people enjoy/don't enjoy road trips? 
In your opinion is summer vacation an important break for children to have away from school? Why/why not? 
Should adults also have a summer break from work?
