Muhammad Ali in a boxing stance. 
Facts:
Ali refused to be drafted for the Vietnam War for religious reasons. He was arrested and banned from boxing for three years.  
As a musician, Ali recorded two spoken word albums and a rhythm and blues song, receiving two Grammy Award nominations from this work.
Ali donated millions to charity organizations and disadvantaged people, and helped to feed more than 22 million people afflicted by hunger.
