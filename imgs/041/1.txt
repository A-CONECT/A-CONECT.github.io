Henry Ford standing next to one of his vehicles.
Facts:
Henry Ford worked as an engineer at the Edison Illumination Company, where he met Thomas Edison.
Henry Ford built his first steam engine when he was only 15 years old.
In 1918, half of all cars in America were Ford Model T's.
