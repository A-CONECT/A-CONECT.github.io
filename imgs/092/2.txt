Facts:
There was a 20% increase in church and synagogue attendance following 9/11.
During the attack, a dog led her blind owner down 78 flights of stairs out of the north tower and safely to a friend's home. 
Upon their completion in 1970/1971, the Twin Towers were the tallest buildings in the world for two years.
