Facts:
Almost 10,000 Red Cross workers have helped people impacted by Hurricane Harvey in Texas and Louisiana.
On average, 26 million people are forced from their homes each year as a result of natural disasters. 
The world responded to the 2004 earthquake, and resulting tsunami in South Asia, by donating more than $13 billion and initiating the largest relief effort in history.  
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some environmental disasters that have occurred in the US? 
What are some safety responses people use when faced with a natural disaster (ex: getting up high during tsunamis, sandbags during floods, standing under a doorway during earthquakes, etc.)? 
Can you think of some useful items to have on hand when a disaster hits (ex: flashlight, generator, canned food)?
(EP)

Did you ever partake in an environmental disaster drill? Where was it? 
Have you donated money to a disaster relief cause? What motivated you to help?
(EX)

Do you think a natural disaster brings a community closer together? Why would someone think otherwise?
