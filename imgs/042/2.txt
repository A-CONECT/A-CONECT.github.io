Facts:
"Take Your Child to Work Day" started out as "Take Our Daughters to Work Day" and was proposed by Gloria Steinem. It aimed to boost the confidence of young girls and decrease school drop-outs.
In 1963, Gloria Steinem became known for her controversial article "A Bunny's Tale" where she went undercover as a Playboy Bunny and exposed the objectifying treatment of women.
Gloria Steinem's magazine was named "Ms." to reflect how women in the 70's no longer wanted to be owned by their fathers or husbands.
