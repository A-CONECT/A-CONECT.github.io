Oprah Winfrey hosting her show, “The Oprah Winfrey Show,” in 1986.
Facts:
There's an "Oprah Effect" that refers to the fact that people who appear on "The Oprah Winfrey Show" or whose products are endorsed by her often find immediate success afterwards.
At the age of 19, Oprah was anchoring for Nashville's WTVF-TV making her the youngest person and first African American woman to hold the position.
Oprah Winfrey is the first African American female billionaire.
