Facts:
Over 50% of Americans said they had shed tears in the four days between Kennedy's death and funeral.
Due to his poor health, Kennedy had to fake his way into the Navy with a false Doctor's note.
One of Kennedy's first presidential acts was creating the Peace Corps.
