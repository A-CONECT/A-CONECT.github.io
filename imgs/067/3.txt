Facts:
The country with the largest mutual fund dollars is the United States followed by Luxembourg and Ireland.
In the early 1970s, a group of Kodak employees asked Congress to allow part of their salary to be invested in the stock market and be exempt from income taxes thus resulting in the 401(k). 
In 1882, Charles Dow and Edward Jones started publishing a two-page summary of the day's financial news called the "Customers' Afternoon Letter". The Dow Jones industrial average is still used today.
