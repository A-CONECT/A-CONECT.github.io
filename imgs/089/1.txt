Facts:
The Sydney Opera House Grand Organ is the world's largest pipe organ and has over 10,000 pipes and took 10 years to build.
The Vegetable Orchestra is an Austrian musical group who use instruments made entirely from fresh vegetables. 
Symphonic House, located in Cathead Point Michigan, emphasizes natural sounds and has windows that can be adjusted to let in the wind in order to imitate a harp in the living room.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some instruments you can think of? 
What is your favorite instrument? Why do you like it? 
Can you think of any famous musicians? What instrument(s) did they play?
(EP) 
Have you ever played an instrument? What was it like? Have you ever taught someone else how to play an instrument? How did that go? 
Have you ever listened to live music (ex: concerts, symphonies, etc.)? What's the most memorable performance you've seen? 
Have you ever seen someone turn an everyday item into an instrument? 
(EX) 
What are some benefits to learning how to play an instrument at a young age? Do you think it might be more difficult to learn an instrument at an older age? Why/ why not? 
Do you think people are born with some musical talent? Can musical talent be learned? 
Do you think music is an effective therapy? How so?
