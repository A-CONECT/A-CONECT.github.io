Facts:
The electric stand mixer was invented in 1908 by Herbert Johnson, an engineer. His inspiration came from observing a baker mixing bread dough with a metal spoon.
Sliced bread and toast have been around since the Roman Empire. At this time, toasting was used as a method of prolonging the life of bread.
Microwaving food requires a short amount of time which maintains more vitamins and minerals than almost any other cooking method.
