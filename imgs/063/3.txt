Facts:
In Scotland, homeowners paint their front door red when they pay off their mortgage.
In New York, it is a legal requirement for a seller to disclose if a property under inspection is believed to be haunted by ghosts.
Detroit, Michigan has a group called the "Mower Gang" who go around beautifying public spaces for free.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What do you consider when choosing a new place to move to? 
What are some of your favorite styles of houses? 
What are some things you look for in a house or apartment? 
(EP) 
Can you recall a neighborhood that you really enjoyed living in? What was it like? Have you ever had a really annoying neighbor? What did they do to annoy you? 
Have you ever been a part of neighborhood watch? 
Have you ever worked on your own house (ex: painting, remodeling, etc.)? What did you do?
(EX) 
Some communities require houses and yards to fit a certain standard, do you agree with this? Why might someone disagree with this? 
What are some pros and cons of renting vs. buying a home? 
In your opinion, is it important to be close with your neighbors? What makes you think this way?
