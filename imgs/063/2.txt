A family moving into a new home.
Facts: 
The average American moves 12 times in his or her lifetime.
More people moved to Texas than any other state in 2016.
The average weight of transported household possessions is 6,500 pounds.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Where do you get boxes for moving? 
What are some common reasons people move? 
How many times have you moved?
(EP) 
When was the last time you moved? How was that experience? How long did it take you to pack? 
Have you ever struggled to move a piece of furniture into a new home? What furniture was it? How did you solve the problem? 
Have you ever helped a friend move? How did you help? 
(EX) 
Some people choose to do all of their moving in one day, while others might move over a few days. Which one do you prefer? Why might someone prefer the other way? 
Can you think of any pros and cons to moving yourself vs. hiring movers? Would you rather move often or live in one place for a while? Why? 
Which activity do you find easier: packing or unpacking? Why?
