Topic: Family ReunionsPicture Caption:
A family of several generations looks out towards their family farm.
Facts:
The world's largest family reunion consisted of just over 4,500 people.
There is an adoption reunion registry that is used to locate parents and siblings and bring families together.
Planning for a family reunion should begin about a year to 18 months in advance. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What activities do people do at family reunions? What would be your ideal family reunion? 
How big are your family celebrations usually? 
Do you have any family that lives out of the country?
(EP) 
Have you ever attended a family reunion? Where was it at? How did you get there? 
Do you have any funny stories from a family reunion? 
Have you ever attended a school reunion? What was that like?
(EX) 
Do you think seeing extended family members regularly is important? Why/why not? 
Are family reunions worth the hassle of scheduling and planning? Why/why not? 
If you could see one person in your family for a day, who would it be? Why?
