Facts: 
In Australia, people celebrate their birthday with fairy bread, a type of sweet bread that’s slathered in butter and sprinkles. 
In Malaysia, people rarely open the gifts they receive in front of others.
The historic origins of wedding anniversaries date back to the Roman Empire, when husbands crowned their wives with a silver wreath on their twenty-fifth anniversary, and a gold wreath on the fiftieth.
