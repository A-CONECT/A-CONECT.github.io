Facts:
In 1946, Congress created the National School Lunch Program. The program helps pay for 5 billion lunches served at schools each year.
According to a 2016 State of School Nutrition survey, elementary schools' lunch periods are, on average, 25 minutes long. 
Lunch boxes date back to the mid-1800s when farm and industrial workers would fill old tobacco tins or sturdy dinner pails with food for their lunches at work.
