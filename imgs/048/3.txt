Facts:
In Ancient Greece, the poor depended on street food for nutrition because they often didn't have ovens in their homes to prepare food. 
Some cultures consider it rude to walk on the street while eating.
Street vendors in Bangkok, Thailand, feed 40% of the population of that city.
