Facts:
According to Guinness World Records, the largest shoe collection is held by one person and contains almost 15,000 pairs of shoes.
Only 1% of the Smithsonian's total collection is available for viewing at any given time.
People often collect items as a way to reduce anxiety, regain a sense of control, and reminisce about the past. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Do you collect anything? Why do you collect this item? 
What are some common collectables you can think of? 
How many items are needed to be considered a collection? 
(EP) 
Can you recall a collection of items at a friend or family member's house? What were they? 
What's the most treasured item you have owned? What made this item so special? 
Have you ever owned an item that became more valuable over time? Did you keep it or get rid of it? 
Have you ever lost or sold an item that you later regretted?
(EX) 
Do you think some people collect too much? Why do you think they do this? Why might this be a problem (e.g., hoarding)? 
Some people collect items only for monetary value but never sell them. Why do you think people do this? How much would you spend on a collectable item? 
Do you think the value of a collectable item is self-imposed or socially influenced? Why?
