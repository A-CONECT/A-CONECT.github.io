Facts:
More money is spent on gardening than any other hobby.
About 20% of all Americans identify as birdwatchers.
Knitting was initially a male-only occupation. The very first knitting union was in 1527 Paris and no women were allowed.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some common hobbies that you can think of? 
Do you have any favorite hobbies? 
Have your hobbies changed throughout your life? How so?
(EP) 
Were there any hobbies that you enjoyed when you were younger? What were they? What did you enjoy about them? 
Do you recall any popular or unique hobbies in your family or community? Tell me about them. 
Have you ever started a hobby that you decided you didn't like? Why didn't you like it?
(EX) 
Do you think modern technology has changed hobbies? Do you think this is a good or bad thing? Why? 
Do you think it's important to stay busy most of the time? Why/ why not? Why might someone think differently? 
What are some positives and negatives to someone making a hobby their full-time job?
