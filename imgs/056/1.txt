Facts:
10% of consumers dress up their pet as a pumpkin.
Wonder Woman was the most Googled Halloween costume in both Portland and Detroit in 2017. 
Trick-or-Treat for UNICEF has helped to raise more than $175 million since 1950. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Have you dressed up for Halloween? What are some of the costumes you've worn? 
Did your children dress up in costumes for Halloween? What are some of your favorite costumes you've seen? 
Have you ever made your own costume or a costume for your children? What was it? 
(EP) 
Do you remember the last time you went or took someone trick-or-treating? Did you have fun? 
What was Halloween like when you were growing up? What is it like now that you're an adult? 
Were there rules in place when you were a child or with your children when it came to going out and trick-or-treating or how much candy you could eat? 
What where they? Have you ever been to a costume party? Tell me about it.
(EX) 
How old is "too old" to trick-or-treat? Why might someone think differently? 
What are some pros and cons to trick-or-treating? 
Pre-made costumes are made widely available each year. Do you think there are some benefits to making your costume from scratch? What are these?
