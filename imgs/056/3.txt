Facts: 
Apple bobbing began as a British courting ritual, where each apple was assigned the name of an eligible bachelor and each woman would try and grab the apple representing the man she was interested in. 
In November, people of Thailand celebrate the Festival of Light that involves people releasing thousands of lit lanterns into the sky while making a wish.
The Arctic Tern bird travels 11,000 miles each way for its annual winter migration.
