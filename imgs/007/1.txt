Facts: 
In 2013, pets outnumbered children by a 4 to 1 ratio in the U.S.
A dog’s sense of smell is 10,000 times stronger than that of a human’s.
Three dogs from First Class cabins survived the sinking of the Titanic.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Have you known any dogs? What were their names? What breed(s) were they? 
Do you have any favorite dog breeds? Which ones? 
Which dog breeds do you see more than others? 
What personality types make people like some dog breeds more than others?
(EP) 
Have you ever seen a dog do something funny? What happened? 
Have you ever met an aggressive dog? Where were you and what did it do? 
Have you ever seen a well-behaved dog? Where were you and what was it like? 
Have you ever trained a dog? How? 
(EX) 
Some people use shock collars or choke collars to help train their dogs. Do you think this is okay? What are some pros and cons of using shock or choke collars? 
Some people are willing to spend thousands of dollars on their dogs, whether for pure breeds or for expensive medical bills. Would you be willing to do this? Why? What are some reasons to do this or not to do this? 
Restaurants, hospitals, some parks and many other places do not allow dogs. Should they? What do you think are some reasons they should or should not be allowed?
