Facts: 
On average, a cat sleeps 12-16 hours a day. 
A cat was the mayor of Talkeetna, Alaska, for 15 years. 
In the 1960s, the CIA tried to turn a cat into a spy by implanting a microphone into her ear. She survived the surgery but got hit by a taxi on her first mission.
