Facts:
The highest point on land, Mt. Everest, and the lowest point on land, the Dead Sea, are both found in Asia.
Christianity, Islam, Hinduism, and Buddhism all started in Asia.
90% of rice on Earth is consumed in Asian countries.

(SM)
What are some regions of Asia that you can think of? 
What are some common animals from Asia?
What are some common food items from Asia that come to mind?
(EP)
Did you learn much about Asia in school? What did you learn?

Was there a time when you found yourself surrounded by what felt like "too many" people? How did you handle the situation?
(EX)

What are some changes you would like to see in your city in the next 10 years?
