Facts:
"La Tomatina" is a festival held in Spain where people throw thousands of tomatoes at each other.
Vatican City is the smallest city in the world by area, occupying only 44 acres. 
'Big Ben,' London's famous clock tower, has its time adjusted every year with an old British penny. If the clock is running fast, a penny is added to the pendulum to slow the clock ever so slightly, if the clock is running slow a penny is removed.
