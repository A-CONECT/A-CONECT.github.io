Facts:
The Maasai people of Kenya and Tanzania have strict policies against killing wild animals.  
Libya was the first African country to declare independence. This fight for freedom inspired the US Civil Rights movement. 
Elephant babies, which are main African safari animals, are often seen sucking their trunks, in the same way that a human baby might suck its thumb.
