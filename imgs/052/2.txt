Facts:
There are at least five towns in the US that have a population of 1.
Southport, North Carolina is a tiny 2.2-square-mile harbor town. People almost exclusively walk, bike, or drive golf carts to get from place to place. 
More residents in small towns own their homes "free and clear" of a mortgage or loan, than urban residents.
