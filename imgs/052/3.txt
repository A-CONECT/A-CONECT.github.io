Facts:
Although he moved soon after his birth, Ray Charles maintained close ties with his hometown of Albany, Georgia. He even included the state song "Georgia on my Mind" in one of his albums.
As of 2017, the city of Portland, OR has the most craft breweries in the country.  
The famous Michigan treat of Vernon's Ginger Ale was created in 1866 by James Vernon, a Detroit pharmacist.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Where were you born? Do you still keep in contact with anyone from your hometown? 
What kinds of activities did you do in your hometown? 
What clubs were offered in your hometown (ex: 4H, YMCA, ect.)?
(EP) 
What do you remember about your hometown? Has anything changed? 
Have you ever been to any events (ex: parades, festivals, etc.) in your hometown? What were they? What do you remember about them? 
Is there anything special or unique that your hometown is/was known for? Tell me about it. 
(EX) 
What are some pros and cons of staying in your hometown versus moving somewhere new? 
Do you think the preference for where someone lives changes throughout their lifetime? Why might someone think differently about this? 
Do you think people benefit more from growing up in one home versus people who had to move around a lot?
