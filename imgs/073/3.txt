Facts:
If we recycled all newspapers, we could save over 250 million trees each year.
For 2012's Earth Day, over 100,000 people from China rode bikes to reduce carbon emissions and fuel use.
One wind turbine can produce enough electricity to power up to 300 homes. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some ways to be environmentally friendly? 
What kind of items can you recycle? 
What are some events that have impacted animals and their habitats (The Exxon spill, Chernobyl reactor, Amazon deforestation)? 
(EP) 
Have you ever been part of a volunteer project within your community? What did you do? 
Are there any activities you do to conserve energy or reduce your impact on the environment? When was the first time you tried this? 
Have you ever been affected by pollution? How so?
(EX) 
How effective do you think our current conservation efforts are in preserving our planet? What are some obstacles to conservation? 
Some people worry that reducing fossil fuels will cost people their jobs. What are your opinions around alternative energy sources (wind mills, solar, nuclear)? 
Do you think one person can make a difference in preserving our environment? Why or why not?
