An Aircraft worker in 1942 during World War II.
Facts:
In 1943, women made up 65% of the U.S. Aircraft workforce.
During World War II, the number of women in the labor force grew by over 6 million.
Due to increased employment of women during World War II, the government funded over a thousand wartime child-care nurseries.

(EX)
