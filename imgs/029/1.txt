Facts:
Alexander Bell, the inventor of the telephone, recommended answering the telephone with "Ahoy".
The U.S. women operators of WWI were known as 'The Hello Girls'. The 223 members connected as many as 150,000 calls per day.
A 2011 survey found that more than half of customers have hung up the phone out of frustration when they could not talk to a real person.
