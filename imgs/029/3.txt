Two women having a video conference over computers.
Facts:
The first Picturephone cost more than $5 a minute to use.
Video conferencing has been estimated to be 80% effective in establishing the same benefits a face-to-face interaction provides. 
In the United States, 1.1 million patients recently reported using video technology to receive medical care.
