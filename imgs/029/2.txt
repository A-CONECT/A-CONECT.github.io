Facts:
In 1920, the world’s first commercial radio station went on the air in Pittsburgh. It was such a hit that four years later, there were 600 commercial stations around the country.  
Country music and Talk radio stations are the two most popular radio broadcasting formats.  
When the car radio was introduced, some states wanted to ban it and argued it could distract drivers and cause accidents.
