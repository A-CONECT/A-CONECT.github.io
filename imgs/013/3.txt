Facts:
The University of Victoria has a class called "Science of Batman" where students study the practical applications of Batman's equipment.
The creator of Wonder Woman, along with The Lasso of Truth, also helped create the first lie detector.
In 2017, the most popular costumes for children were action and superhero characters.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Do you have a favorite superhero? Why do you like them? 
Have you ever wanted a superpower? What was it? If not, what would you say your superpowers or best traits are? 
Were your children interested in superheroes growing up?
(EP) 
What was the first superhero you can remember hearing about? How did you hear about them? Have you seen any of the recent or past superhero movies (ex: Batman, Superman, Wonder Woman, etc.)?  What did you think of them?
(EX) 
Why do you think people like superheroes so much? What about them draws people into their stories? 
In your opinion, are superheroes healthy for children to look up to? Why or why not? Do you think superheroes set unrealistic expectations for children? What do you think these are? 
Superheroes are predominantly male and white, what do you think about this?
