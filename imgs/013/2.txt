Facts: 
Marvel, a major comic book company, nearly went bankrupt in the late 1970s, but was saved by starting a “Star Wars” comic series.
Last year the comic industry made over 1 billion dollars in revenue. 
According to ”the Comics Code” of 1954, the hero had to win at the end of the comic book for it to be sold by newsstands.

(SM) 
Did you or anyone you knew growing up have a comic book collection? Did/do you have any favorites? 
Have you noticed any changes in comic book content over time?
(EP)
