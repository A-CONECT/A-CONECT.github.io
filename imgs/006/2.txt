A young woman performing in the Guadeloupe Winter Carnival wearing a traditional carnival outfit. 
Facts:
It is predicted that by 2055, the US will not have a single racial or ethnic majority. 
More than 300 languages are spoken in the US. 
Every 33 seconds a person immigrates into this country.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Do you still have family in other countries? Which ones? Have you visited them or have they visited you? 
Do you know anyone who has lived in another country? How do you stay in touch? 
What other countries would you like to live in? Why? 
What other cultures have you experienced? What do you think is valuable in other peoples' culture?
(EP) 
Have you ever encountered a cultural tradition that was unfamiliar to you (e.g., holiday, ceremony, artistic performance, etc.)? What was that like? 
What are some interesting things you have learned about other cultures? How did you learn them? 
Have you ever eaten any unfamiliar food? What, when, and where? What was your reaction?
(EX) 
What are some of the benefits of cultural diversity in a country? What challenges might diversity bring? 
What do you think is the best way for a person to immerse themselves in another culture? What are some of the benefits to experiencing other cultures?
