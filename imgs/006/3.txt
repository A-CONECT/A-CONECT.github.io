Topic: "The American Dream"Picture Caption:
Recent immigrants waving American flags. 
Facts:
Even after adjusting for inflation, most Americans will make more money than their parents.
About 36% of children born in the US to immigrants graduate from college, which is 5% more than the national average. 
FDR summarized the American Dream using the four essential freedoms: freedom of speech and religion, freedom from want and fear. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Have you heard the term "The American Dream”? 
What is your idea of "The American Dream”? 
What was your parents' idea of "The American Dream”? 
How did your family build a life for themselves? 
(EP) 
Do you remember any particular hardships your family went through when you were younger? What were they? How did you come out of it? 
Was there a particular achievement in your pursuit of ”The American Dream" that stands out to you? What was it? Did you set a new goal after that one was completed?
(EX) 
Is the idea of ”The American Dream" outdated in your opinion or is it something that changes as society changes? 
Do you think other countries should adopt "The American Dream”? How would this be beneficial in other countries? How would it not be beneficial? 
Do you think that there are enough resources in this country for everyone to achieve their "American Dream”?  Why or why not?
