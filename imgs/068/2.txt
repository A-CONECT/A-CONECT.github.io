Facts:
There’s a small house movement that’s getting a lot of attention. The typical American home is around 2,600 square feet, whereas the typical tiny house is less than 400 square feet.
8 out of 10 non-homeowners indicate that owning a home is part of their American Dream.
In a 2013 Community Preference survey, almost 60% of home buyers said they would give up a larger yard in favor of a shorter commute.
