Facts:
Grilling and smoking are family traditions. Almost two-thirds of grillers/smokers learn it from a family member.
A man in Georgia holds the record for longest barbeque. For 80 hours he barbequed hotdogs and corn to benefit the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
Researchers have found the average kebab provides 98% of the daily salt quota for an adult.

(SM) 
Do you know how to grill/BBQ? Do you use a charcoal or gas grill?

Do you think there's a difference between barbequing and grilling?
