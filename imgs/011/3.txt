Facts:
The average American owns 7 pairs of blue jeans.  
South Korea used to have actual fashion police who would go around measuring the lengths of women's miniskirts. If skirts were deemed too short, the woman wearing it could be fined or arrested.
During WWII, the potential threat of gas warfare influenced the fashion industry to create attractive safety solutions such as a handbag that included compartments for gas masks.
