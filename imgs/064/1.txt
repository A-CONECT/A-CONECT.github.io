Facts:
The first-ever commercial microwave stood nearly six feet tall and weighed 750 pounds.
The first bristle toothbrush was invented in China in 1498. 
Duct tape was originally named "duck tape" because of its waterproof nature until the tape became used for broken air conditioning ducts; the name then changed to "duct tape".
