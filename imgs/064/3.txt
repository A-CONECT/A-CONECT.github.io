Facts:
The largest fridge in the world is 30 kilometers long and actually did not store any food, but was created for scientists to understand the universe.
The tire company Goodyear has been developing tires that rely on magnetic levitation and hover beneath the car, instead of being bolted on.
In 1936, Russia built a computer which ran on water to solve complex mathematical equations.
