Facts:
Fox Team members from The Michael J. Fox Foundation have raised over $65 million for Parkinson's research.
19-year-old Charlotte Woodward attended the first women’s rights convention in the United States, and was the only person there that lived long enough to see women's suffrage become legal. 
The first Earth Day took place on April 22 1970, and marks the birth of the modern environmental movement.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some national volunteering organizations you are familiar with? 
How could someone get involved in your community? 
How has bringing awareness to important causes change over the years?
(EP) 
Have you ever volunteered before? What was that experience like? 
Have you ever been personally assisted by an organization? What was that experience like? 
Do you know anyone who has worked for an important cause? What did they do?
(EX) 
Should elementary schools require students to regularly participate in volunteer programs? Why/why not? 
Are there important causes that you think have done harm accidentally? How so? 
Do you think certain causes or organizations attract more attention and money than others? With the rise of social media, do you think that it has become easier to bring awareness to causes people find important?
