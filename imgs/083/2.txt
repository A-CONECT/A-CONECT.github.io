Facts:
A study showed that meditating at least 40 minutes a day slows the rate of aging in the brain. 
Meditation is scientifically proven to overcome stress, improve digestion, and alleviate anxiety. 
The typical brain comprises about 2% of the body’s total weight, but uses 20% of its total energy and oxygen intake.
