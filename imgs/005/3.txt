Facts: 
President Andrew Jackson's opponents called his supporters "jackasses." This then became the symbol of the democratic party. 
The Republican symbol of the elephant came from an 1874 political cartoon. The cartoon showed an elephant falling into a pit, predicting a Republican electoral defeat.
Throughout US history, many third parties have made brief appearances during elections. Some of these parties include the Anti-Nebraska Party and the American Vegetarian Party.
