Facts:
Since 1950, Oscar winners have only been allowed to sell their award if they offer it to the Academy for $1 first.
Alfred Hitchcock's acceptance speech is the shortest in Oscar's history. He said, "Thank you".
Saturday Night Live has won the most Emmys of any individual TV show. As of 2017, it has had 64 wins.
