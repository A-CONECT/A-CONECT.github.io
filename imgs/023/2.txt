Facts:
The Soccer World Cup has been stolen twice, once from its exhibition in London and then again from Brazil. It is still lost to this day.
Michael Phelps, a US swimmer, is the most decorated Olympic athlete with a total of 28 medals, 23 of which are gold. 
The trophy given to the winner of the Super Bowl (Football) is created by Tiffany and Co. and uses 7 pounds of pure sterling silver. 
Possible Questions:
(SM)
What sports have you played? Did they hand out awards for that sport? 
Do you watch any professional sports? What awards are given out in that sport? 
What sports was your school known for?  
(EP)
Have you ever attended a sports competition/tournament? What was it like?
Did you know someone who was really good at a sport? What sport did they play? Did you ever watch them play a game? 
Can you remember attending any sports award ceremonies or banquets?  
(EX)
What are some pro/cons of being a competitive person? 
Do you have to win a game to feel a sense of victory? Why or why not? Why might someone think differently? 
How might an athlete's performance be effected as they get closer to achieving an award? How might the pressure to perform enhance or weaken their performance? 
Some events offer awards to all participants – What are some positives and negatives to this?
