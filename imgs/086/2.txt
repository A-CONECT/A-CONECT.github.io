Facts:
Dorothy Dandridge was the first African American nominated for the Best Actress Academy Award, for her 1954 performance in the opera-based production of "Carmen." 
The University of Oregon is "Faber College" in the comedy movie "Animal House," and was the only school to allow the film to be shot on their campus.
The opening sequence of "Beverly Hills Cop" was filmed in Detroit, and includes real life Detroit police officer Gil Hill as Axel Foley’s boss.
