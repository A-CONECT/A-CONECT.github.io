Facts:
The Exorcist (1973) has been voted the scariest movie of all time. 
In addition to being Stephen King's first published novel, Carrie was also the first of his stories to be made into a film. 
The character of “Dracula” may have been based on the authors’ terrible boss. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some famous monsters from scary movies you are familiar with? 
What is something that always scared you as a child? 
Are you familiar with any true events that have inspired scary movies?
(EP) 
Have you ever had a nightmare after watching a scary movie? What happened? 
Have you ever seen a scary movie that caused you to scream or cover your eyes? What movie was it? Do you remember what was happening in the scene? 
Do you remember going on a date to see a scary movie? How did the date go?
(EX) 
Why do some people find being frightened appealing? Why do other people avoid scary movies? 
Do you think people are more afraid of evil creatures or evil humans in movies? Does this change with age?
Should parents restrict their kids from watching scary movies until a certain age? Why or why not?
