Facts:
In the movie Titanic, the crew had only one chance of getting the grand staircase flood scene completed because everything was destroyed in the process. 
Bill Murray and John Travolta turned down the role of Forrest Gump. Travolta later admitted passing on the role was a mistake. 
"Play it again, Sam" is one of the most widely quoted Casablanca lines, but it is misquoted. In the film, the saying is "Play it once, Sam".
