Facts:
The Beatles were the first entertainment act to perform at a large stadium concert, which sold out in 17 minutes. 
The Beatles' music was banned in the Soviet Union, which made acquiring their vinyl very difficult. People worked around this by imprinting music onto used X-Ray scans. 
The Beatles refused to play at their concert in Florida until the audience was desegregated and all attendees were treated equally.
