Topic: Nat King Cole & Breaking BarriersPicture Caption:
Nat King Cole and his daughter Natalie Cole pose for a portrait session in 1955.
Facts:
At 15, Nat King Cole dropped out of school to become a jazz pianist full time. 
In 1991, Natalie Cole achieved a hit song when she recorded a duet with her father by adding her vocals to his song "Unforgettable." It won seven Grammy awards.
Nat King Cole performed at John F. Kennedy's presidential inauguration in 1961.
