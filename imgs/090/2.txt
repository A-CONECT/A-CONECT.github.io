Elvis Presley performing in Las Vegas in 1969.
Facts:
The most popular stamp is the 1993 U.S. commemorative stamp of Elvis Presley.
In 1954, Elvis auditioned for a gospel quartet named The Songfellows and was rejected.
Milton Berle reportedly advised Elvis against performing live with his guitar, so he did a hip thrust on screen instead which led to his nickname "Elvis the Pelvis."
