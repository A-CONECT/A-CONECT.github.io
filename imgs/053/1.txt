Facts:
Starbucks, the biggest coffee chain in the world, was founded in Seattle, Washington.
Some of the giant redwood trees in California are over 2,000 years old.
Portland, Oregon ranks as the "Greenest City" in the United States and is the second most bicycle-friendly city in the world.
