Facts:
Ohio has a larger Amish population than any other state. 
The Union Pacific’s Baily Yard in Nebraska, is the world’s largest railroad yard with an area of 2,850 acres. 
Colon, Michigan, is known as the Magic Capital of the World. The small city of 1,100 people manufactures more magic supplies than anywhere else.
