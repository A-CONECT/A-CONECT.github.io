Facts:
"Uno" is considered to be the most popular card game played in the U.S.
Alzheimer's research has linked mentally stimulating activities, such as card games, with larger brain volumes and better cognitive scores. 
American Bryan Berg broke a record after spending 44 days completing the largest playing card structure, using just under 220,000 cards.

(EX)
