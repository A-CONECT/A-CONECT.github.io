Topic: Popular Board GamesPicture Caption:
Residents in a Virginia living community play a game around values and changes they want in their community.
Facts:
The longest game of Monopoly lasted 70 days.
Operation was invented by a sophomore at University of Illinois in 1962 as part of a class assignment to invent a game or toy.
One-in-three American households has a Scrabble set.

(SM) 
What are some board games you can think of? 
What are some common pieces that are used with board games?

(EP)

(EX)
