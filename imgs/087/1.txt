A couple attends one of Oregon's four remaining drive-in movie theaters.
Facts:
The largest drive-in theater is in Dearborn, Michigan and holds up to 3,000 cars.
There are currently about 330 drive-in theaters in the United States compared to a peak of about 4,000.
The Newberg, Oregon drive-in theater is family owned and has been in continuing operation since 1953.
