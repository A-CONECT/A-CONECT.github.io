Facts:
Popcorn became a popular movie theater snack during WWII when sugar rations made candy less accessible.
Box office revenues have showed that comedy is the most popular movie genre for more than 20 years.
The first movie theater was opened in Pittsburgh in 1905 and had a 5 cents admission price. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What was the last movie you saw in theaters? Who did you see it with? 
Do you think there are movies/movie types that are meant for "the big screen"? What are they? 
What are your favorite movie snacks?
(EP) 
Was there a popular movie theater in your town growing up that everyone went to? 
Do you remember the first time you went to a movie theater? What did you see? 
Have you ever been to a movie premiere? What was that like? 
Did you ever sneak snacks into a movie theater? Did you ever get caught? 
(EX) 
Do you think it is important to consider those around you when in a movie theater? Why or why not?
What do you think about bringing a young child or baby into the movie theater? 
Would you rather have the movie theater to yourself or do you enjoy sharing the space? Why might someone think differently?
