Facts:
Slightly more than half of the respondents to a survey stated they preferred watching movies at home than going out to a movie theater.
U.S. consumers are spending an estimated total of $2.1 billion per month on television and movie streaming services.
In the 1970's, the first American films released on VHS included "The Sound of Music" and "Patton".

(SM) 
What are some home movie viewing technology you can think of (ex: TV, VHS, DVD, etc.)? 
What home movie viewing technology do you have at home? 
What is the last movie you watched at home?
(EP) 
Do you remember when your family first got a VCR? 
Did you go to video stores to rent movies? 
What kind of movies did you usually watch? 
Did your friends or family have regular movie nights? 
When did you first hear about or experience streaming movies? 
Overall, how have you seen home movie viewing changed over your lifetime?
(EX)
