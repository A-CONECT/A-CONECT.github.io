Facts:
The first road-worthy cars used a lever instead of a steering wheel to steer. It had a design and functioning like that of a joy stick.
Mary Anderson invented the first windshield wipers in 1903, which were hand-operated at the time.
The first electric car was made in 1905. However, due to low gas prices at the time, the combustion engine took over.
