Facts:
Around 90% of Americans consume more sodium than health experts recommend for a healthy diet.
Americans have tried to lose weight an average of seven times in their lives.
Weight Watchers was founded in 1963 by Jean Nidetch when her neighbor assumed she was pregnant rather than overweight.
