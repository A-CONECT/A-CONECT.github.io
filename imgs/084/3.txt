Facts:
Regular exercise stimulates the creation of new neurons (cells) in the brain, just like the rest of your muscles. 
Exercise can keep skin looking young, and may even reverse the effects of aging in people who start working out later on in life. 
Endorphins released from exercise have the ability to provide pain relief better than morphine.
