Anne Frank in Montessori School in 1940, Amsterdam. 
Facts:
Anne Frank and her family lived in a secret annex for over two years to avoid concentration camps. This house is now the Anne Frank House museum and has millions of visitors each year.
A 2013 article found over 80% of girls in their late-teenage years write about their lives in a private notebook. 
Anne Frank and Martin Luther King Jr. were both born in 1929.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Have you read the Diary of Anne Frank? 
What are some different topics that diaries/journals cover? 
Do you know any other books written in this era (1940s)? 
(EP) 
Did you keep a diary as child? What type of things did you write in it? 
Did you take any measures to hide your diary from others? How did you hide it? 
What was your perception of the holocaust when you were young? Did your perception change over time? How?
(EX) 
Do you think it's important to remember people like Anne Frank? Why? Do you think there are people that are overlooked for their accomplishments in times of war? 
What makes Anne Frank's story inspirational to you? Must a person go through hard times to be inspirational to others? Why or why not? 
Would you want to have an autobiography or movie based on your life? Why or why not?
