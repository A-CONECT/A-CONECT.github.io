Facts:
Billie Jean was the first music video by a black artist to air on MTV. 
Thriller is the best-selling album of all time. It sold 42.4 million copies. 
Michael Jackson has 39 Guinness Book of World Records, one of which is for supporting the most charities as a Pop Star.
