Actor Tom Cruise gets ready to do his own stunts for the fourth "Mission: Impossible" movie.
Facts:
Tom Cruise in "Mission: Impossible Ghost Protocol" did his own stunts when he scaled the tallest building in the world with suction cups on his hands. 
Stuntman Hal Needham, was once the highest-paid stuntman in movies and was so good that he was the first person to ever test air bags.
The tornado in the "Wizard of Oz" was a woman's silk stocking strung up and twirled around by a fan.
