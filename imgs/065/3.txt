Facts:
Four-year-old Xavier Hames is the first patient to receive an artificial pancreas to manage his type 1 diabetes.
The fermentation process (like the brewing of beer) was the first time biotechnology was used to convert a food source into another form.
A boy in South Africa was able to get 3D-printed prosthetic fingers in 20 minutes for $10; compared to traditional prosthetics which can cost over ten thousands of dollars.
