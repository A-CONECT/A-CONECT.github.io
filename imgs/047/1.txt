Facts:
The world's first breakfast cereal was created in 1863 and needed to soak overnight to be chewable.
Kellogg recently admitted all Froot Loops are made from the same flavoring concoction known as "Froot".
In Ancient Arab culture there was only one way a woman could legally divorce, and it was if her husband didn't provide enough coffee.
