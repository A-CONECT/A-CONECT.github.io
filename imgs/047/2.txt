Construction workers having lunch in uptown Charlotte, North Carolina.
Facts:
According to the National Peanut Board, the average American child will eat 1,500 peanut butter and jelly sandwiches before they graduate high school.
The most popular office lunch in America consists of pepperoni pizza, chocolate ice cream, skittles, and coke.
Brazil serves one of the healthiest school lunches in the world. It includes wholesome fresh foods, such as black bean and rice salad, green salad, stir-fried meat dish, and sometimes baked plantains for a "sweet" component.
