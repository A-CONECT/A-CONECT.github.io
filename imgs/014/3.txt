Facts:
The biggest bookstore in the world is the Barnes & Noble located in New York City. It's about 150,000 square feet and includes more than 12 miles of shelves.
There are libraries around the world where you can check-out humans as a living book and listen to their stories. There are 150 such libraries around the world.
In 2007, famous author Stephen King was mistaken for a vandal when he started signing books during an unannounced visit to a bookshop in Australia.
