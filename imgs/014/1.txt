Facts:
Research has shown that reading helps people relax more than having a cup of tea, listening to music, or going for a walk.
Theodore Roosevelt read, on average, one book a day. He continued this trend while he served as President.
The three most commonly read books in the world are: The Holy Bible, Quotations from Chairman Mao, and Harry Potter.

(SM) 
Do you read any materials for enjoyment? 
What are or were some of your favorite subjects to read about? 
Have your reading interests changed over time? 
Has the content that's available to read (ex: being published) changed over time? 
(EP)

Was there ever a book that you read that you didn't enjoy? 
Have you ever been a member of a book club or attended an event centered around reading?
(EX) 
Do you think reading for pleasure is a good use of a person's time? Why do you feel this way?
