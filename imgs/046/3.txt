Chef Andre Chiang preparing food at Restaurant Andre in Singapore.
Facts:
Having a limited menu that is constantly changing allows the chef to buy local, seasonal items when they are at their peak freshness.  
It is typical that ladies order first and are served first at fine dining establishments. 
One of the priciest dining experiences in Japan is at Kitcho, where a meal here will run close to $600 per person.
