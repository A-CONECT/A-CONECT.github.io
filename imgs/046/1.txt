Facts:
Due to the large amount of McDonald's restaurants, you are always within 115 miles from a McDonald's in the continental USA.
In comparison to the rest of the world, people in Hong Kong, China are considered the most frequent visitors of fast food chains.
The popularity of the drive-thru led to the installation of cup holders in cars in the 1990's; and as fast food drinks became larger, so did the cup holders.
