Topic: Single ParentsPicture Caption:
A single Mother helping her son with his homework.
Facts:
Nearly three out of ten families with children today are headed by a single parent. 
Males are the fastest growing category of single parents.
The United States has the highest percentage of single-parent families among developed countries, followed by Canada and then Australia. 
Possible Questions:(SM)
How might raising a child on your own might be challenging? 
What are some potential differences in parenting styles between single parents and couples? 
What are some important resources when it comes to raising a child on your own? 
(EP)
Do you know anyone who was a single parent? Who were they? 
Were you ever raised by just one of your parents (even briefly)? What was that like? 
Would you say you are more similar to one of your parents? How so? 
Which of your parents were you closest to? Why do you think you got along so well?
(EX)
Today, people who want to have a kid on their own have a variety of options (adoption, in-vitro fertilization, etc.). Do you think there should be certain requirements for a person to take on this commitment on their own? Why/why not? 
Divorce rates are much higher now than in the past. Why do you think this is? Can you think of some potential benefits to divorce? 
What are some pros (e.g., stronger bond with child) and cons of being a single parent?
