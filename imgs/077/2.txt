Four women firefighters geared-up for service.
Facts:
Amelia Earhart the first woman to fly solo across the Atlantic Ocean and was the sixteenth woman to ever receive a pilot’s license.
During World War II, Danish fishermen secretly ferried over 90% of the country's Jewish population to safety in neutral Sweden.
Chile is the only country in the world where all firefighters are volunteers.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some professions that demand more courage than others? 
What are some common acts of courage? 
What is one of the most incredible acts of courage you have heard about?
(EP) 
What is the most courageous thing you have done? 
Have you ever taken a risk for a career or personal goal? What was the risk? How did you muster up the courage to take that step? 
Have you ever influenced someone else to be more courageous? What happened?
(EX) 
Does it take courage to achieve certain life goals? How so? What goals might require this? 
Do you have to be fearless to be courageous? Why might someone think differently? 
Do you think obstacles can bring about courage or growth? Is it easier to be courageous when an individual faces no obstacles? Why or why not?
