Facts:
Ashima Shiraishi, a Japanese-American female, is considered the world's best rock climber and has been excelling at this sport since she started at age 6.
According to research, biking a few times a week reduces blood pressure and stress.
A 100-year-old man from India is the oldest marathon finisher in the world. He finished the race in 8.5 hours.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What outdoor activities do you like to do? 
Are there any you don't enjoy? 
What is your preferred season for outdoor activities? 
Do you like to do these activities alone or with others?
(EP) 
Have you learned how to ride a bike? Who taught you and when? What was the process like? 
What outdoor activities did you do growing up? 
Is there an outdoor activity that you've tried that you didn't enjoy? 
(EX) 
Do you think outdoor recreation is accessible to the entire public? Can you think of any obstacles to this? 
Do you think it should cost money to use local and national parks/green spaces? If so, how much? What should this money be used for? How could this be problematic? 
Do you think outdoor recreation should be mandatory for youth? Why/why not?
