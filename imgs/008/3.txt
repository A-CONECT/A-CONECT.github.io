Facts: 
Elephants have massive trunks that weigh around 400 pounds, but they still have enough control over their trunk to pick up a single grain of rice.
Although racehorses can live up to 30 years, no racehorse over the age of 18 has ever won a race.
Over the course of the television series "Lassie," six different dogs played the lead role.
