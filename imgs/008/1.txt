Facts: 
On average, a person consumes almost 600 pounds of dairy annually.
The heaviest turkey ever raised weighed 86 pounds, which is about the size of an average third-grader.
Sheep wool has natural UV protection, which helps prevent sheep from getting sunburnt. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What types of farm animals can you think of? 
What is your favorite farm animal? 
Are there any farm animals you don't like? 
What are some responsibilities that come with raising farm animals?
(EP)
Did you know anyone that worked on a farm growing up? What do you remember about their farm? 
Have you ever had a bad experience with a farm animal? What happened? 
Do you have any memories of visiting a farm or petting zoo? What was it like? 
Have you ridden a horse? What was that like?
(EX) 
Do you eat meat or/and dairy products? How do you feel about the treatment of farm animals raised for meat or dairy consumption? Can you think of possible improvements or alternatives?
What do you think of diet choices that exclude meat or dairy? What are some positives or negatives to these diets? 
Which environment, city or country, do you think is most beneficial to raise kids? What might be some benefits or drawbacks to growing up in each of these places?
