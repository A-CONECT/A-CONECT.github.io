Facts:
There is a Butterball Turkey Talk Line that answers turkey-related cooking questions from November to Christmas Eve. This line receives almost 100,000 calls each season.
The first TV dinner was a result of Swanson overestimating the amount of frozen turkeys it would sell for Thanksgiving by 26 tons.
According to the American Pie Council, 6 million men ages 35-54 admitted to eating last slice of pie, but denying it when asked.
