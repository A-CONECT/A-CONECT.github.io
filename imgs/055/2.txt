Facts: 
At the University of Wisconsin, the most popular Thanksgiving tradition is "Frozen Turkey Bowling" where people hurl frozen, oiled-up turkeys down a grocery store aisle at a set of bowling pins. 
The "wishbone" originated in ancient Italy. People believed that birds could predict the future, so they would pick up the bone and make wishes on it. 
"Friendsgiving" is a gathering of friends over a Thanksgiving feast. It is thought that this tradition may have started among college students who could not easily travel home for the holiday.

(SM) 
What are some common Thanksgiving traditions?

Do you think traditions add to family gatherings? What are some pros/ cons to having traditions?
