Facts:
The first Thanksgiving dinners did not include turkey. Instead, they had deer, lobster, and oysters which were plentiful and cost less at that time.
Alcatraz in California has held an "Unthanksgiving Day" since 1975 to give thanks for the survival of the Native Americans after the settlement of Europeans. 
The first Thanksgiving Day football game took place in the mid-1870s when Princeton played Yale in Hoboken, New Jersey.
