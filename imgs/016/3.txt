Facts:
Virginia is currently the top ranked state to retire to due to its high quality of life and a low cost of living.
Compared to the overall population, retirees spend more time reading, working on home projects, and relaxing.
Of volunteers serving through a religious organization, 40% are ages 65 and over.
 Possible Questions:
(SM) 
When did you retire? 
What was your last job before you retired? 
How did you prepare for retirement? 
Can you think of any jobs that were known for having good retirement plans? Has this changed throughout your lifetime? 
(EP) 
How did you celebrate your retirement? 
What was the first hobby you picked up when you retired? Tell me about it. 
What have been your most enjoyable moments since retirement? 
Have there been any times since retirement that you missed working? What made you feel this way? Did you find any solutions?
(EX) 
Some people choose to work well past the age of retirement. Why do you think some people get bored if they don't work? 
Should retirement benefits support a person's lifestyle or just focus on providing the basic necessities? Why might someone think differently? 
What do you think the retirement age should be? Why? Why would someone think it should be younger or older?
