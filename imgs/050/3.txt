Facts:
Gambling is such a huge industry in Nevada that even the state prison had a casino inside it for 35 years.
Macau is the only Chinese territory where it is legal to gamble in a casino and it also happens to be the world's largest gambling city. 
In some Asian cultures, the number 4 is thought be bad luck. For that reason, some hotels in Vegas have no floors that start with the number 4.
