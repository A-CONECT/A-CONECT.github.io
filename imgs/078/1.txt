A Mother soothing her baby with a lullaby.
Facts:
Studies revealed that pet owners feel they receive just as much support from their pets as they do from their family members. 
Lavender essential oil is said to have calming, sedative effects. 
It was found that males preferred hearty comfort foods, such as steak and casseroles, while females instead preferred chocolate and ice cream. 
Possible Questions:
(SM)
Why might someone need to be comforted? 
What are things that people can do to show they care? 
Who are some people you turn to for comfort?
(EP) 
Can you recall a time when you had to comfort someone in a time of need? Were you able to help them? 
Have you felt supported by your family? When did you feel supported? 
Are there any activities you've done in the past to comfort yourself? What activities did you do?
(EX) 
Why do some people prefer physical comfort while others prefer to have space in stressful times? 
Is it a good thing to rely on others for comfort? Why/why not? Can you think of any examples? 
People often rely on things or activities (ex: quilting, pets, etc.) to comfort themselves. What are some pros and cons to this?
