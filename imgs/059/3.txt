Facts:
July Fourth is the "biggest hot dog holiday of the year" with Americans consuming about 155 million of them.
More than one-fourth of Americans will purchase patriotic clothes for the 4th of July.
For India's independence day, it is tradition for children and adults to competitively fly kites and try to cut each other's kite strings loose.
