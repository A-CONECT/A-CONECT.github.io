Facts:
Sparklers can burn as hot as 2000 degrees Fahrenheit.
The U.S. Army is trying to design environmentally friendly fireworks for military use.
The American Pyrotechnics Association (APA) estimates that more than 14,000 professional fireworks are displayed each 4th of July.
