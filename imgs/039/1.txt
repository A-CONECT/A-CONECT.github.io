Topic: SiblingsPicture Caption:
Two siblings playing in the garden with one another.
Facts:
According to Time magazine, kids spend more time with their siblings than anyone else.
A University in Italy found that twins start bonding in the womb as early as 14 weeks old.
Two of the best women tennis players today are the Williams sisters. They've played against each other 24 times in tournaments.
Possible Questions:
(SM)
Do you have any siblings? How many years apart were you in age? 
Do/did you have any friends that were like your sibling? 
Did you get along with your siblings? 
If you don’t have a sibling, did you ever want one? Why?
(EP)
Did you ever get into trouble with a sibling? How did that go? 
Have you ever "tattled" on a sibling? 
Has your relationship with your sibling(s) changed over time? How so? 
Is there anything that your sibling has taught you? What was it?
(EX)
In your opinion, do parents play favorites with their children? What leads you to think this? 
What are some pros and cons to treating siblings the same vs. differently? 
How do you incorporate an adopted or step sibling into the family? Should they be treated differently at all? 
Does having a sibling provide certain benefits (e.g., life lessons)? What about being an only child?
