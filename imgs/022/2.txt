Facts:
The Rose Festival parade held in Portland, Oregon, is the second largest floral parade in the U.S. with more than half a million people lining the parade route. 
Each year during Oktoberfest, the German beer festival, over 5,000 items wind up in the lost found. These items have included wallets, passports, and even a pair of dentures. 
The African World Festival, Detroit’s largest ethnic festival, is a celebration of the worldwide influence of African culture through performances, cuisine, and fashion.
