A paperboy delivering the morning newspaper. 
Facts:
Roughly 1 in 3 Americans worked in a restaurant for their first job. 
In 1978, Nearly 60% of teens had summer jobs, the highest rate of teen summer employment. 
Warren Buffet's first job was delivering papers for The Washington Post, of which he is now a major shareholder.  
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
How old were you when you started your first job? What was it? 
Did you have a dream job? 
Did you have any side jobs (ex: babysitting, paper route, etc.)? 
Do you think there are certain jobs that provide valuable experiences? 
(EP) 
How did you get your first job? Why did you choose that particular job? 
What was your favorite job you've ever had? What was your least favorite job? 
When did your children get their first jobs? 
Have you seen a change over the years in how people approach their jobs or careers? 
(EX) 
Do you think it is more important to work in a job that you feel strongly about or one that pays more money? 
How do you feel about pursuing your passion when you have a family to support? 
Do you think that the overall work ethic of Americans has changed over the years? What changes have you noticed?
