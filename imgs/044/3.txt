Facts:
"Philo" means love and "sophia" means wisdom. So, "philosophy" means the love of wisdom.
Gandhi's book "An Autobiography of My Experiments with Truth" was declared one of the '100 Most Important Spiritual Books of the 20th century'.
Socrates loved to debate, but he often played ignorant to gain a better idea of what others were thinking.
