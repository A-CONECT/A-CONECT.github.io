Topic: AuthorsPicture Caption:
Author, Maya Angelou, reciting her poem, “On the Pulse of Morning,” at President Bill Clinton’s inauguration in 1993.
Facts:
"On the Road" by Jack Kerouac was typed on one continuous roll of paper. It was 120 feet long.
William Shakespeare introduced 1,600 words into the English Language.
Maya Angelou never attended college but received more than 50 honorary degrees in her lifetime.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Who are some famous authors you can think of? 
What popular books or stories have stuck with you over the years? 
Do you have a favorite author? What about them makes them your favorite? 
Were there any authors you learned about in school? Which ones?
(EP) 
Have you ever been to a book signing? Who was the author? 
Have you ever been to a poetry or book reading event? Was there anything that stood out to you (ex: books/poems that you enjoyed hearing aloud)? 
Did you or anyone you know ever publish any work? What was it? 
Did you ever start anything you hoped would be published one day? 
(EX) 
Why might an author write a book under a pseudonym or different name? What are some pros and cons you can think of for doing this? 
In your opinion, should an author write for their audience, or for themselves? Why might someone think differently? 
Do you think that now, an author's work is held to a higher or lower standard than in the past? What makes you think this way?
