Facts:
The U.S. Treasury says that Americans hold about $15 billion in loose change. 
The first chartered savings bank in the United States was the Provident Institution for Savings in Boston, incorporated in 1816. 
The Federal Reserve estimates more than 24 billion checks are written in the U.S. every year.
