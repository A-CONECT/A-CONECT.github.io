Facts:
The Apollo Astronauts, unable to qualify for life insurance and not insured by NASA, resorted to ‘insurance autographs’, signing just before launch in the expectation that the value would skyrocket in the event of their death. 
65% of American families have some type of life coverage, totaling $19 trillion dollars. 
According to a nationwide study conducted by LearnVest and Guardian, over a third of respondents didn't know what kind of policy they had, or felt unsure that they would know how to collect a payout.
