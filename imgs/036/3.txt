Facts:
MTV went on air at midnight, August 1, 1981 and the first video was The Buggles’ memorable “Video Killed the Radio Star.”
In 1972, jazz legend Ella Fitzgerald was the first African-American woman to sing during the Super Bowl halftime show.
The film, "The Sound of Music," had the longest first run in US cinemas ever at four and a half years.
