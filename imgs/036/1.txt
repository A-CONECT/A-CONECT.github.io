Topic: Local ShowsPicture Caption:
A band playing at a local Jazz Club in New Orleans.
Facts:
Before Stevie Wonder was discovered at the age of 11, he would perform on the street corners of Detroit. 
In 2016, South African Broadcasting Corporation announced 90% of the music they will play will come from local artists. 
Studies show that listening to music can lower blood pressure and improve moods. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What kind of music do you enjoy watching live? 
What are some popular concert venues in your community? 
What is your favorite concert venue? 
Are you a fan of local artists in your area?
(EP) 
Did you or anyone you know play in a band locally when you were growing up? 
Were there any local high school bands in your town? Tell me about them. 
What was the first live local music show you went to? 
Were there bars or clubs that had live music where you frequented often? 
Did you ever perform music in a local show?
(EX) 
What are some benefits and costs to attending music shows? 
Do you think it’s important to have a variety of music offered locally? Why? 
Do you think local music is supported as much as it should be? Why/ Why not? What else can be done to support local musicians?
