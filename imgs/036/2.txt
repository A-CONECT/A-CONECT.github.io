Facts:
In 1985, two Live Aid concerts raised over 200 million dollars for famine relief in Ethiopia.
A poll from MSN UK found less than half of the people attending music festivals go for the music.
Woodstock included 32 acts performed outdoors before an audience of more than 400,000 people.
