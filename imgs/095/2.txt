Facts:
The oldest person to climb Mt. Everest is an 80 year-old man from Japan.
The highest known mountain on any planet in the Solar System is Olympus Mons on Mars at nearly 70,000 feet.
The longest mountain range is the Mid-Ocean Ridge, which is around 40,000 miles long and 90% of it is under the ocean.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Which well-known mountains can you think of? 
What are activities you can do on a mountain? 
Do you know of any active volcanos? Which ones?
(EP) 
Have you ever wanted to climb a mountain? Why/Why not? 
Have you been near a mountain? What was your experience like? 
Have you or someone you know been in an outdoor mountain relay (ex: Hood to Coast, Big Bay Relay)?
(EX) 
Why do you think some people brave harsh and dangerous conditions to climb mountains such as Everest? Should thrill-seeking activities be prohibited? Why/why not? 
Do you think mountain rescue services should be financially responsible for people who ignore posted warning signs? 
Is it important to protect mountains and their surrounding areas from things like open pit mining, drilling, coal, etc.? Do you think they have valuable resources that we should utilize? Why/why not?
