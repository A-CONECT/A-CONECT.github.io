Facts:
One of the reasons your lungs feel refreshed when walking through a pine forest is because of an anti-inflammatory compound called a-Pinene, found in conifers.
The oldest known living organism is an 80,000-year-old tree that looks like an entire forest on its own with its numerous branches and roots.
Even though only 1% of rainforest plants have been studied for medicinal properties, more than 25% of the medicines we use originate in rainforest plants.
