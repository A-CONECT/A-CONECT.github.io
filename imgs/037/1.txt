Facts:
The average American will be a grandparent for half of his/her adult life.
75% of grandparents use the internet, and that percentage is continually rising.
Grandparents spend $52 billion every year on their grandkids alone.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Were you close with your grandparents? Did they live close to you? 
Did you spend a lot of time with your extended family? 
Did you spend more time with your mother's or father's side of the family?
(EP) 
Did your grandparents participate in raising you? How so? 
Did you help raise any grandchildren? What did you do to help raise them? 
What are some memorable moments you recall with your grandparents? 
Do you have a memorable moment about being a grandparent?
(EX) 
Sometimes family members have different values. Did your parents have different views than your grandparents? How so? Why do you think that happens? 
Do you think it is important for children to meet and spend time with their extended families? What are some of the pros and cons of this? 
In your opinion, has the role of a grandparent changed over time? Why and in what ways?
