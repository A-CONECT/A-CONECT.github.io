Facts:
Every sweater that Mr. Rogers wore in Mister Rogers' Neighborhood television show was knit by his mother.
A study found parents enjoy their kids' company most around the age of 5. The age parents least enjoyed was 11.
Since 1970 the average age of first time moms in America has increased by 4 years. 
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What were your parents' names?
Are you or any of your siblings named after your parents? 
Were you closer to one of your parents? 
What did your parent's do for a living? 
What important values did your parents pass on to you?
(EP) 
What were your parents like when you were growing up? 
What were some house rules for you as a child? 
What were some house rules for your children? 
Can you recall a specific moment between you and your parents where you saw each other as equals? Was there a similar moment you had with your own children? What did that feel like?
(EX) 
In your opinion, what makes someone a parent or parental figure? Why do you think this?
When is the best age to have children? Why? 
Did you see your relationship change with your parents as you entered adulthood? How so? Does everyone's relationship with their parents change in this way? Why or why not?
