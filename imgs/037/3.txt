Godparents and parents surrounding newly baptized baby at St. Vincent’s Church in Australia.
Facts:
Traditionally, a child was expected to have three godparents. Two were to be of the same sex as the child and one of the opposite sex.
In Latvia, it is common for the godparents to choose the child's name rather than the parents. 
Prince William and Kate's son, Prince George, has seven godparents.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
Did you have godparents? 
Did your children have godparents? 
What are some of the roles of godparents? 
What are some good characteristics for a godparent?
(EP) 
Were you ever asked to be a godparent? Tell me about that. 
Were there any other role models or figures in your life other than family? Who were they? 
How did you choose godparents for your children? 
If you did not choose godparents, why did you decided not to? 
(EX) 
Do you think godparents are an important part of a child's life? Why/why not? 
What are some of the pros and cons of having non-related people closely involved in a child's upbringing? 
Do you think godparents are more or less common these days? What makes you think so?
