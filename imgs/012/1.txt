Several couples dancing. 
Facts:
In 2008, Sweden installed a "sustainable" dance floor. Dancing on this floor produces energy that lights up the floor tiles.
The longest dance marathon ever recorded was 123 hours. 
A three-hour ballet performance requires the same amount of energy as running about 18 miles.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some dance styles you can think of? 
What were some popular dances when you were growing up? Are these dances still popular today? 
What kinds of music did you like to dance to? Is that music still popular today?
(EP) 
Did you ever learn to dance? 
How old were you when you learned? 
How did you learn to dance? 
Where would you go to dance? 
Who would you dance with? 
Have you ever been to a ballet? Tell me about that.
(EX) 
Do you think that dancing styles have changed? If so, how? What are some good things about these changes? Is there anything you don’t like about these changes? 
Many people say that dancing is a form of expression. With that in mind, can someone be a "bad" dancer? Why do you think that? What are the pros and cons of using dance as a means of expression, even if someone is a "bad" dancer? 
Dance-based workout videos and aerobic dance classes are popular. What are the pros and cons of using dance as a form of exercise?
