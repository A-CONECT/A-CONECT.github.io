Facts:
It's a tradition to eat fried chicken on Christmas in Japan, especially KFC's fried chicken. In total, people in Japan request about 3,000,000 orders of KFC each Christmas.
Many of the traditional foods eaten during Hanukkah are fried in oil. This is because this Jewish holiday celebrates the miracle of one night's worth of oil lasting for eight nights.  
Kwanzaa gets its name from a Swahili phrase meaning "first fruits of the harvest," and families traditionally decorate their households with fresh fruits.
