Facts:
In Tokyo, people commonly go to KFC for their Christmas meal. People will book their meal a couple months in advance to secure a seat at a KFC and enjoy a bucket of fried chicken. 
A study by Food Network UK found it takes a person 47 years, on average, to cook a Christmas dinner without any mistakes. 
Fruit cake is one of the most re-gifted items ever in the history of Christmas foods.
