Facts:
Monika Lewinsky hid out at her mother’s room in the Watergate Hotel during the political scandal involving President Bill Clinton. 
A former Senator of Oregon was accused of sexual harassment, and forced to resign after his diary was found. The Senator tried to edit the most incriminating passages, but was unable to hide his actions—as detailed later in his diary.
In 1922, a former Secretary of Interior accepted bribes from big oil companies in exchange for drilling rights. This scandal was called the "Teapot Dome" due to the teapot shaped land that was involved in the agreement.
