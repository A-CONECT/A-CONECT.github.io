West Germans celebrate the fall of the Berlin Wall atop what remains on Nov. 12, 1989.
Facts:
President Reagan and Soviet Leader Gorbachev agreed to pause the Cold War in case of an alien invasion, though the specifics aren't known.
During the Cold War, Chrysler built air raid sirens powered by V8 engines. They could be heard for 16 miles and remain the loudest sirens ever built.
A part of the Berlin Wall One is in a men's room of the Main Street Casino in Las Vegas.
