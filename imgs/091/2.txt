Facts:
On October 4, 1957, the Soviet Union started the space race by launching the Sputnik 1, the first Earth satellite in history.
What shocked every single astronaut who walked on the moon was its distinctive and stinky smell.
There are about 1,000 active satellites orbiting the earth, plus there are about 2,000 that no longer work.
