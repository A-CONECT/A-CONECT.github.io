Facts:
The San Diego Padres baseball team has for over twenty years signed a contract with Matt LaChappa, a physically disabled ball player, so he doesn’t lose his health insurance; he is known as being a "Padre for life". 
Netherlands is the only country in the world where it is compulsory for everyone to have private health insurance.
In the U.S. we spend more than any other country on healthcare at $2.8 trillion annually--more than $8,500 per person.
