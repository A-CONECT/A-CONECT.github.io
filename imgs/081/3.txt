Facts:
About 40 million Americans avoid seeing the dentist out of fear and anxiety.
France is rated #1 by the World Health Organization for their mixed private-public system, resulting in no waiting lists to see doctors.
Americans are as misinformed about their own health care system as they are about systems in other countries.
