Facts:
The very beginning of country music can be traced to folk songs played by immigrants that settled in the Appalachian Mountains. 
In the 1920s, Atlanta's music scene played a major role in launching country's earliest recording artists.
Since country artist Dolly Parton was 7-years-old, she has written approximately 5,000 songs.
