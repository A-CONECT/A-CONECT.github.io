Facts:
Louis Armstrong is considered to be the 'Father of Jazz Music'. He loved performing so much that some years he would perform up to 300 concerts.
Jazz musicians have their own words to describe their music, for instance, a "cat" is a jazz musician and a "hot plate" is a great recording of a song.
The piano trio is the most common jazz configuration with a piano, bass guitar, and drums which take full advantage of swing, interaction, and dynamics.
