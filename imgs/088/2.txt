Facts:
"Rock Around the Clock" by Bill Haley and the Comets, which brought rock and roll into the mainstream, was initially a commercial disappointment until it was made popular when used in a movie.
Bob Dylan's "Like a Rolling Stone" is considered to be the greatest song in classic rock because it was the first song to break the 3 minute limit on FM radio.
Aretha Franklin is tied with Stevie Wonder for the most number one Rhythm and Blues songs.
