Facts:
80% of vision problems worldwide are avoidable or curable. 
Due to our curved corneas, our retina processes things upside down and our brain turns the image the correct way. 
Over 500 years ago women in Italy used to put an extract called Belladonna on their eyes to dilate their pupils and attract men. Belladonna literally means "beautiful lady".
