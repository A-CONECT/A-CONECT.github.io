Facts:
The middle ear contains the three smallest bones in your entire body which all together can fit on a penny.
35 million Americans have impaired hearing.
After English and Spanish, American Sign Language is the third most used language in the United States.
Possible Questions:
(SM) 
What are some common treatments for ear infections? 
Do you know anyone that wears a hearing aid? 
What are some animals that have an acute sense of hearing?
(EP) 
Have you ever been to a loud event? Did you wear ear plugs? 
Have you ever misheard someone that resulted in a funny misunderstanding? 
Are there any noises that you don’t enjoy (ex: traffic, snoring, etc.)? How do you respond to them? 
(EX) 
Should teaching sign language be a part of the elementary school curriculum? How would this affect the students? 
"Selective hearing" is when people choose what they actively hear. Do you think people can control what they selectively hear or is it an involuntary response to noise? 
Historically, the Deaf Community was against cochlear implants, fearing for the loss of their culture. Do you think parents of deaf children should decide if their child receives a cochlear implant to hear, or should a medical power of attorney, acting in the benefit of the child, make the decision?
