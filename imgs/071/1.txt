Facts:
A British study revealed that individual cows form “best friendships” with others cows. When separated from their “best friend,” their stress levels increase and the milk yields decrease.
According to a study at the University of Chicago, babies can understand social relationships, especially friendship, before they can walk or talk. 
The longest friendship bracelet made measures over 2,000 feet long.
