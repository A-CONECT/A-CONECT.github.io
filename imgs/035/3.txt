Facts:
As of 2017, a new law requires traveling circuses in the U.S. to use only human entertainers or shut down permanently. 
The hugely successful Cirque du Soleil has never used animals in its shows, but instead relies solely on the talents of their human performers.
The first circus elephant was initially purchased to plow her owner's fields. However, after eating too much of the field crops, she was driven around as a spectacle, which became the first traveling circus.
