Facts:
In 1881 nurse Clara Barton brought supplies and support to soldiers in the field during the Civil War. This later became the Red Cross.
If a cell phone has been barred from making outgoing calls (e.g., not paying a bill) often emergency calls can still be made.
The National Fire Protection Association reported only 4% of their assistance was for actual fires while 64% was for medical aid.
Possible Questions:
(SM)
What types of emergency services are you familiar with? 
What kind of situations require emergency services? 
What organizations respond to emergency situations (Red Cross, Doctors Without Borders, etc.)? 
(EP)
Did your hometown have a paid fire department or were they volunteers? Did you ever volunteer? 
Have you ever been in a situation where emergency services were required? What was that like? 
Have you ever been involved with an organizations providing services in a time of need? What was your role? If not, do you know someone who has?
(EX)
Do you think your taxes are being spent wisely for emergency services? Would you be willing to pay more for better services? 
Can donations be more of a hindrance than a help during a disaster? How might that impact relief organizations? Are there organizations that take advantage of dire circumstances?
